evolIA turns your genuine mobile activity into a personal "cognitive value" signal.

Instead of tracking you for someone else's profit, evolIA keeps everything on your device and measures real engagement — messages, photos, video, physical motion, and nearby WiFi/Bluetooth presence — to compute a value that grows with how you actually use your phone.

Features:

* Offline-first: your data stays on the device, no account, no server.
* Sensor fusion: a linear-acceleration motion signal (gravity removed) plus RSSI-filtered WiFi/BLE proximity, so only real, nearby interaction counts — not ambient noise.
* Cognitive value model: an evolving formula rewards meaningful activity.
* Optional peer-to-peer mesh: share signed value blocks across your own devices, each carrying a cognitive proof-of-work so values cannot be fabricated.
* Optional on-chain anchoring: anchor your verified value to a blockchain when you choose to — it degrades gracefully when unused.
* Adaptive defense: the more injection or forgery attempts it absorbs, the harder it throttles them — strictly reactive, it never retaliates, and it relaxes again when the pressure stops.

evolIA is free and open source software (MIT). The Android app is self-contained Kotlin and runs without any companion service.
